## Competitor Intelligence

- **Norton Critical Editions** — gold standard but slow on new PD titles, no direct channel.
- **Penguin / Oxford** — mass market, light annotations, purely retail.
- **Standard Ebooks** — free PD, no annotations. Not direct competition.
- **SparkNotes** — study guides only, not annotated reading editions.
- **Gap:** No major player combines annotated physical book + digital companion + community/membership stack. This is the moat.
